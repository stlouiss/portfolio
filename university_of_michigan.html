The Master of Science in Information (MSI) program at the University of Michigan is a professional graduate program with a flexible curriculum and a strong emphasis on teamwork, client-centered career development opportunities, and applied learning.

Transcending disciplinary boundaries, the program encourages intellectual risk-taking and serendipity, making it possible to experience deep personal transformation while serving others.
