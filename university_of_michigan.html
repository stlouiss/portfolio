Master of Science in Information (MSI) Program

Focus on User-Centered Digital Curation

August 2019 to April 2021 (expected)



The MSI program at the University of Michigan focuses on professional growth. The program has a flexible curriculum, as well as a strong emphasis on teamwork. Opportunities for applied learning and client-centered career development are abundant.

Transcending disciplinary boundaries, the program encourages intellectual risk-taking and serendipity. Students can thus experience deep personal transformation while serving others.
