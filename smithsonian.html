National Numismatic Collection

Smithsonian Institution,

National Museum of American History

Summer 2015



As an intern at the National Numismatic Collection, I worked in one of the world's largest collections of monetary objects.

A major highlight of the experience was contributing to cataloging projects. One such project was for the Josiah K. Lilly, Jr. Collection, consisting of various gold coins and bars minted in the United States and around the world.

Additionally, I pursued face-to-face networking opportunities while living in Washington, DC. I met with experts at the Institute of Museum and Library Services, the National Archives and Records Administration, and the Library of Congress. These experts focused on archives and records management, federal grantmaking, digital preservation, and public history programming.
